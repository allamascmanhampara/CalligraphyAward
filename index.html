Jury's Message

It has been an absolute honor to witness the remarkable talent and dedication displayed by all participants in the Nuqtah Calligraphy Awards 2025. Each entry reflects not just skill, but a deep reverence for the art of Arabic calligraphy. The precision in letter formation, the thoughtful compositions, and the creative expressions presented here demonstrate the bright future of this timeless art form. Every participant should be proud of their work, as each piece carries its own unique beauty and significance. May this competition inspire you to continue perfecting your craft and preserving this noble tradition.

Khaleelullah Chemnad
Chief Jury
